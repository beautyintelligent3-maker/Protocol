## 1. Getting Started & Passwords

### Logging In
To access BIW OS, visit the login page and enter your registered email address and password. If you do not have an account, you must contact the Owner or HR to have one created for you.

**Initial Owner Account (Default Credentials):**
- **Email:** `alice@example.com`
- **Password:** `password123`
*(Note: Please change this password immediately after your first login!)*

### Changing Your Password
If you know your password but want to change it:
1. Log in to your Dashboard.
2. If you are on a **Desktop**, click the **Key icon** at the bottom of the left sidebar.
3. If you are on a **Mobile device**, tap the **Profile** button in the bottom navigation bar, then tap **Change Password**.
4. Enter your new password and save.

### Forgot Password
If you cannot log in:
1. Click the **"Forgot password?"** link on the login screen.
2. Enter your email address.
3. Check your inbox for a recovery link from BIW OS.
4. Click the link to securely set a new password.

## 2. Roles & Permissions

The BIW OS relies on three core roles:

*   **Owner**: Has absolute access. The Owner can see all rooms, all tickets, and is the only person who can access the "Staff Management" portal to add/edit/deactivate employees.
*   **HR**: Has elevated access. Can approve/reject tickets requiring HR approval and can manage inter-departmental issues.
*   **Employee**: Standard access. Can only see and access the Rooms (departments) they have been explicitly assigned to.

## 3. The Ticketing System

The core of BIW OS is the Ticketing system, which allows you to assign tasks, request approvals, and track progress.

### Creating a Ticket
1. Navigate to the specific Room where the ticket belongs.
2. Click the **"Create Ticket"** button.
3. Fill out the Title, Description, Priority (Low/Medium/High), and set a Due Date.
4. Assign the ticket to a specific staff member in that room.
5. If the ticket requires HR approval (e.g., budget requests, leave requests), check the **"Requires HR Approval"** box.

### Ticket Statuses
*   `open`: New ticket, hasn't been started.
*   `in_progress`: The assignee is actively working on it.
*   `approved`: If it required HR approval, it has been granted.
*   `resolved`: The ticket is complete.

### Real-time Communication
Click on any ticket to open the **Ticket Details Panel**. Here, you can chat with your team in real-time. You don't need to refresh the page—new messages will appear instantly. You can also attach files up to 50MB (supporting high-resolution iPhone photos).

## 4. Rooms (Departments)

Rooms organize communication and tickets by department, branch, or project.

*   You will only see the Rooms you are a member of.
*   The system includes pre-defined rooms like "IT Support", "HR", and specific "Branch" rooms.
*   Tickets belong to Rooms, keeping workflows isolated and secure.

---

## 5. The Penalty System

To ensure efficiency and accountability, BIW OS includes an automated **Penalty System**.

Penalties are automatically applied to a Staff Member's account under two conditions:
1.  **Overloaded**: The staff member currently has **more than 5 active tickets** assigned to them.
2.  **Deadline Missed**: The staff member has a ticket that is past its assigned `Due Date`.

If an employee has an active penalty, their status will turn red, and a warning icon will appear next to their name across the system. 
*How to remove a penalty?* The employee must resolve their open tickets or complete their overdue tasks to drop back under the penalty thresholds.

## 6. Staff Management (Owner Only)

If you are the Owner, you have access to the **Staff Management** panel (accessible via the left sidebar).

### Adding an Employee
1. Go to Staff Management.
2. Click **"Add Staff"**.
3. Enter their Name, Email, and assign a temporary Password.
4. The system will automatically generate a permanent, sequential **Staff ID** (e.g., 0001, 0002).
5. Assign them to their initial Rooms.

*Note: Staff IDs are permanent. Even if an employee is deactivated, their ID is permanently tied to their records and will not be reused.*

### Managing Existing Staff
*   You can edit an employee's roles and room assignments at any time.
*   If an employee leaves BIW, you can **Deactivate** their account. They will lose access immediately, but all their past tickets and messages will be preserved for historical records.
